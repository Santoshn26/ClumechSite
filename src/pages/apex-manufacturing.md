### The Challenge

Apex Manufacturing's invoice payment cycles were averaging 85 days, severely impacting operating cash flow. The core issue was the administrative delay in getting verified proof-of-delivery (POD) back to the finance department. This made it difficult to counter invoice disputes and confirm delivery for payment processing.

### The Approach

Clumech integrated its platform with Apex's existing ERP system. Delivery drivers began using a simple mobile app to capture time-stamped, geo-tagged photos as POD the moment a shipment was delivered. This data was instantly and automatically linked to the corresponding invoice in the ERP, making it available to the finance team in real-time.
