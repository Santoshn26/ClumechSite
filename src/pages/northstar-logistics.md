### The Challenge

NorthStar Logistics, a leader in high-value global freight, was facing a critical visibility gap. Once a container left their facility, they were reliant on sporadic updates from third-party carriers. This led to dozens of manual check-in calls per day, increased customer anxiety, and an inability to proactively manage potential delays at ports and transfer hubs.

### The Approach

Clumech deployed its Intelligence Layer, integrating directly with NorthStar's primary ocean, rail, and ground carriers via API. We augmented this data with our own low-power satellite trackers on high-value containers. Geofencing alerts were established around major ports and customs zones, automatically notifying stakeholders of arrivals and departures without any manual intervention.
